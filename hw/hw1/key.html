Key Elements



Typeface


Typeface is the overall type "family" where the letters will share a specific design. For example Garamond, Times, and Arial are typefaces, not fonts.



Fonts


For example, Arial is a typeface; 9pt Arial Bold is a font. A specific style of typeface with a set width, size, and weight.



Tracking


Tracking is the space between letters horizontally.



Serifs vs sans serifs


There is no research the shows whether serif or sans serifs are better to use. However, sans serif are better and easier to read on the web screen. Sans serifs are recommended for the body of the copy and serifs for the title and subtitles.



Leading


Leading is the space between the baslines of fonts vertically.
